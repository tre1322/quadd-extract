Logging In

What You See First

After logging in, you'll see two big boxes. The left box is where you work, and the right box shows your results. At the top of the left box, you'll see tabs that let you switch between different tasks.

The 3 Main Tabs

‚ú® Creating Your First Template

What's a template? A template teaches the computer how YOU want your documents to look. It's like showing someone an example of the perfect way to do something!

1

Click on the "Learn New" tab at the top of the left box.

2

Give your template a name. Pick something you'll remember, like "Hockey Stats" or "Basketball Scores".

3

Type what kind of document this is. For example: "hockey", "basketball", or "honor_roll".

4

Now for the important part! You have two choices:

  • Upload a PDF: If your documents are PDFs, click to upload one example PDF
  • OR paste text: If you copy data from a website, paste it in the big text box
5

Show the computer how you want it to look. In the "Desired Output Format" box, type or paste EXACTLY how you want the final result to appear.

6

Click the big "Learn from Example" button. This usually takes 30-60 seconds. You'll see a loading message.

7

Success! When it's done, you'll see a green message saying it worked. Your template is ready to use!

Example: Hockey Stats
Let's say you copy hockey stats from a website that look messy like this:

Player G A PTS
Smith 2 3 5
Johnson 1 1 2

In the "Desired Output" box, type how you want it to look in your article:

Top Scorers:
- Smith: 5 points (2 goals, 3 assists)
- Johnson: 2 points (1 goal, 1 assist)

‚ö° Using a Template

Once you've created a template, using it is super easy!

1

Click the "Use Template" tab at the top.

2

Pick your template from the dropdown list. Click the dropdown and select the template you want to use.

3

Give it your new document. Just like when you created the template:

  • If it's a PDF template, upload your new PDF
  • If it's a text template, paste your new text
4

Click "Extract Data". Wait a few seconds while the magic happens!

5

See your results! The formatted output appears in the right box. Click "Copy Output" to copy it, then paste it wherever you need it!

üí° Tip: You can use the same template over and over again for the same type of document. Create it once, use it forever!

üéØ Tips and Tricks

‚úÖ The better your example, the better the output.
When creating a template, make your "Desired Output" example as detailed and complete as possible. Show the computer EXACTLY what you want!
‚úÖ If output looks wrong, edit your template.
Go to "Manage", click "Edit Example Output" on that template, and make your example more specific. Then try again!
‚úÖ Each template is for ONE type of document.
Make a separate template for each different type of document you work with. For example, one for hockey stats, another for basketball scores, another for honor rolls.
‚úÖ Give templates clear names.
Use names like "Hockey Box Scores" instead of "Template1". You'll thank yourself later when you have lots of templates!
‚úÖ PDF or Text? Stick with one!
If you created a template with a PDF, always use PDFs with that template. If you created it with pasted text, always paste text. Don't mix them!

üÜò Common Problems

Problem: "I can't see my templates in the dropdown!"
‚Üí Make sure you're logged in. If you are, try clicking the Refresh button. If you still don't see them, you might not have created any templates yet - go to "Learn New" to create one!
Problem: "The output looks wrong or messy!"
‚Üí Go to "Manage", find your template, and click "Edit Example Output". Make your example output more specific and detailed. Show the computer exactly what you want!
Problem: "It says 'Learning failed' or 'Extraction failed'!"
‚Üí This usually means the document is very different from your example. Make sure you're using the right template for the right type of document. If it keeps happening, try creating a new template with a better example.
Problem: "I forgot my password!"
‚Üí Contact your administrator (the person who created your account). They can reset your password for you.
Problem: "It's taking forever to process!"
‚Üí Learning new templates and extracting data takes 30-60 seconds - this is normal! Make sure you have a good internet connection and just be patient. You'll see a loading message while it works.

Still need help? Ask your administrator for assistance!
